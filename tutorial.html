How To Play

The game’s premise is simple – match two of the same cards. But successfully doing so is where the difficulty lies in this game.





On every level, the enemy and the cards will not be the same. The more you go the more cards will appear.








There is also hearts that is given to you, You will only have 3 hearts while the enemy will have more the more you go.





You also have to look out for the timer when the enemy will attack. Each attack of the enemy will decrease your life by 1 and when you match all the cards the enemy's life will decrease by 1.



And ENJOY!
